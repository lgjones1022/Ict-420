About Me

I am a 22-year-old college student from Lexington, Kentucky, currently pursuing a Bachelor of Arts degree with a focus in Business & Organizational Communication. In addition to my major, I am also earning a certificate in Innovation and Entrepreneurial Thinking. My academic background has provided me with a solid foundation in both communication theory and practical business strategies, enabling me to effectively bridge the gap between organizational needs and innovative solutions. As a communicator, I have a deep understanding of the role language plays in shaping group identities and facilitating meaningful dialogue. I’m especially interested in how communication can drive organizational change and foster inclusive environments. My coursework and research have allowed me to explore both local and national political dynamics, helping me develop a nuanced perspective on the intersection of politics and communication. I am passionate about using my skills to help organizations improve their internal and external communication, enhance team collaboration, and develop strategies that drive growth and innovation. Whether through thoughtful public health messaging or navigating complex business environments, I am always looking for new ways to apply my communication expertise in impactful, creative ways.
